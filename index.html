FootBall Dice Game

This game is for Two or More players and uses 6 dice to play.

Keep Score and the first Player to 100 wins.

Number of Sides on DiceFunction of Dice
4 Sides Rolled First1 or 3 = Run Play, 2 or 4 = Pass Play
6 Sides Rolled Second1 or 3 - play is good, 2 or 4 - Dropped Ball, 5 + Run Play - Fumble, 6 + Pass Play - Interception
8 Sides Rolled Third with Run PlaysNegitive yards for Running plays
20 Sides Rolled Third with Run PlaysPositive yards for Running plays
10 Sides Rolled Third with Passing PlaysNegitive yards for Passing plays
30 Sides Rolled Third with Passing PlaysPositive yards for Passing plays
